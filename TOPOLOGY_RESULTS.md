# Cross-topology generalization (torus + open + walls)

Trained on mixed topologies: torus (wraparound), open (clamped boundary),
walls (torus + internal walls). 20 envs per topology = 60 train envs total.
Each batch trajectory samples a random topology + random env.
Eval on held-out instances of each topology separately.

Each topology requires different position-tracking strategy:
- Torus: cumulative angle wraps naturally
- Open: position clamps at boundary, action may be no-op
- Walls: action may be no-op at walls, breaking smooth path-integration
