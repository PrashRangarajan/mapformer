# Hex / place-cell emergence in prediction-trained models

Classical neuroscience test: do trained cognitive-map models show grid-cell-like
(hex-periodic firing) or place-cell-like (peaked at one cell) representations,
analogous to entorhinal cortex and hippocampus?

Procedure:

1. Frozen prediction-trained lm200 checkpoint
2. Run trajectories on training env, record (agent_pos, hidden_state) per step
3. For each unit, build per-cell rate map across visits
4. Compute Sargolini-style grid score (peak correlation at 60° & 120° minus 30° & 90° & 150°)
5. Report frac of 'grid cells' (score > 0.3) and 'place cells' (peak ratio > 5x)

Settings: 100 trajectories × T=256 steps. Multi-seed (n=3 lm200 checkpoints).
