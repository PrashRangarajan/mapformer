**Caveat on cross-class (torus + DoorKey):** TEMFaithful's forward uses
`tokens < n_actions` to distinguish actions from observations. The cross-class
unified vocab has env-prefix tokens (0, 1) and 11 action tokens spread over
ids 2–12, so this binary split breaks. A TEMFaithful row in MULTICLASS_*
would require a custom is-action mask. Skipped here.
