# Cross-env-class generalization: torus + DoorKey

Trains MapFormer variants on a 50/50 mix of torus envs and MiniGrid-DoorKey-8x8 envs.
Tests whether a single cognitive-map architecture can handle:
- Different action vocabularies (torus 4 cardinal vs DoorKey 7 incl. pickup/toggle)
- Different obs spaces (torus full-cell vs DoorKey egocentric)
- Different optimal strategies (path-find vs key/door manipulation)

Unified vocab via disjoint ranges + env-class prefix token at trajectory start.
30 train envs per class, 30 held-out test envs per class.
