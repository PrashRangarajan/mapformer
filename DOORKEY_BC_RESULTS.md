## Interpretation

DoorKey requires three chained sub-tasks: navigate to key, pickup, navigate
to door + toggle (with key), navigate to goal. Total ~15-25 actions for
optimal solution on 8x8. Tests whether the cognitive map supports compound
sub-goal sequencing on a real env (vs our torus-only result).
