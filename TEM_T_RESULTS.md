# TEM-t baseline vs MapFormer (full comparison)

TEM-t = Whittington 2022 transformer formulation of TEM. Parameter-
matched to MapFormer-EM (~250K total). Differs from MapFormer-EM only
in the position-encoding mechanism: sequential `e_t = ReLU(e_{t-1} W_{a_t})`
vs parallel `θ_t = ω · cumsum(f_Δ(x_t))`. This is the empirical comparison
the MapFormer paper claims structurally but never benchmarks.

Single-seed runs across clean / noise / lm200 regimes, evaluated at OOD T=128 and T=512.
